Overview

What is the Attendance Tracker?

A simple web app for teachers to record whether each student is present, late, or absent each session. No paper, no spreadsheets — just open the site and mark.

Every tab

Entering your PIN

Every tab is locked with your teacher PIN. The default is 1234 — change it in Settings before your first session. Enter it once per tab, per session.
